The Outer Wilds Solar System

Explore the mysteries of the Timber Hearth system.

Timber Hearth

The lush home planet of the Hearthians, filled with forests, geysers, and ancient ruins. It's where your journey begins.

Brittle Hollow

A fragile planet constantly shedding its crust into a black hole at its core. Exploration here requires careful timing.

Hollow's Lantern

The volcanic moon orbiting Brittle Hollow, actively erupting and contributing to its parent planet's disintegration.

Giant's Deep

A vast ocean planet shrouded in clouds, home to powerful cyclones and submerged islands. Watch out for the anglerfish!

Ash Twin

A desert planet constantly being stripped of its sand by its twin, Ember Twin, revealing hidden structures.

Dark Bramble

A mysterious, thorny planet filled with dense fog and terrifying predators. Navigation here is a true test of courage.

The Sun Station

An ancient Nomai construct orbiting the sun, shrouded in mystery and vital to understanding the time loop.
